Lasagna

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Lasagna has changed in Italy over the centuries, depending on the areas where it is being prepared. However, Americans have added our own favorite ingredients to lasagna.
